Mrs. Karthika B Nair

Master of Arts in English Language and Literature

Former SDE at ASAP Kerala

Our classes are aimed at an overall development of students in English language and literature. Creative methods of teaching enhance better comprehension of topics like grammar. Effective planning of lessons and regular evaluations are remarkable features of our center. Discussions with parents regarding student’s performance are being conducted every month. Classes are student friendly with activity oriented lessons.


Starting at Trivandrum in 2012, Karthika's tuition center is currently working in Pathanamthitta district of Kerala. We offer classes in English for:

Classes from 1 to 12 (Kerala, CBSE, ICSE Syllabus) Bachelor of Arts (All Universities) Master of Arts (All Universities) UGC National Eligibility Test (NET)
